Privacy Policy

Last Updated: March 18, 2025

Introduction

Power Serve Ministries ("we," "our," or "us") operates the Father Trucker website (the "Site"). We respect your privacy and are committed to protecting the personal information you share with us. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and safeguard your information when you visit our website or interact with our services.

Please read this Privacy Policy carefully. By using our Site, you consent to the practices described in this policy.

Information We Collect

We may collect the following types of information:

  • Personal Information: When you sign up for our newsletter, contact a chaplain, or interact with our forms, we may collect your name, email address, and other contact details you provide.
  • Prayer Requests: Any prayer requests or spiritual concerns you share with us.
  • Usage Information: Information about how you use our website, including pages visited, time spent on pages, and other browsing patterns.
  • Device Information: Information about the device you use to access our site, including browser type, IP address, and operating system.

How We Use Your Information

We use the information we collect for various purposes, including:

  • Providing and improving our ministry services
  • Sending newsletters and updates about our ministry
  • Responding to your requests and inquiries
  • Praying for specific needs you've shared
  • Connecting you with chaplains or other support resources
  • Analyzing website usage to improve user experience
  • Maintaining the security and functionality of our website

Information Sharing

We respect your privacy and will not sell, trade, or rent your personal information to third parties. We may share your information in the following circumstances:

  • With service providers who help us operate our website and services (such as MailChimp for newsletter distribution)
  • With chaplains and ministry staff who provide spiritual support (with your consent)
  • When required by law or to protect our rights

MailChimp/Newsletter Subscription

We use MailChimp to manage our email communications. When you sign up for our newsletter, your information is stored and processed by MailChimp. Their privacy practices are governed by their own privacy policy, which you can review at https://mailchimp.com/legal/privacy/.

Cookies and Tracking Technologies

Our website may use cookies and similar tracking technologies to enhance your browsing experience. Cookies are small files stored on your device that help us improve functionality and understand how you interact with our site. You can set your browser to refuse cookies, but this may limit some features of our website.

Your Rights and Choices

You have certain rights regarding your personal information:

  • Access and update your personal information
  • Request deletion of your data (where applicable)
  • Opt-out of communications (each email we send contains an unsubscribe link)
  • Express preferences about how we use your data

To exercise these rights, please contact us using the information provided at the bottom of this policy.

Data Security

We implement reasonable security measures to protect your personal information from unauthorized access, alteration, or disclosure. However, no internet transmission is completely secure, so we cannot guarantee the absolute security of your data.

Children's Privacy

Our website is not intended for children under 13 years of age. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If you believe we have collected information from a child under 13, please contact us so we can remove the information.

Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy periodically to reflect changes in our practices or for other operational, legal, or regulatory reasons. The date at the top of this policy indicates when it was last revised. We encourage you to review this policy whenever you access our website to stay informed about our information practices.

Contact Us

If you have questions or concerns about this Privacy Policy or our data practices, please contact us at:
